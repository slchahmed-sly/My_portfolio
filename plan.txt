Frontend Design Specifications - Phase 1
1. Design Language & BrandingTheme: "The Professional" — Clean, trustworthy,
engineering-focused.Color Palette:Primary Background: Pure White (#ffffff)
.Primary Text: Dark Slate / Charcoal (#1e293b).Accent/Action Color: Ocean Blue (#0ea5e9).
Brand Gradient: Royal Blue (#2563eb) $\to$ Cyan (#06b6d4).
2. Component SpecificationsA. The Navbar (The Floating Capsule)Form & Position:A minimalist, 
wide, pill-shaped container (fully rounded ends).Floating: Fixed at the top center of the 
viewport, hovering slightly below the browser edge (not touching the top).
Appearance: Solid White background with a subtle drop-shadow to separate it from the content.
 No glassmorphism/blur effects.Elements (Left to Right):Logo: Text-based logo reading "Souleimane".
 Style: Gradient text effect using the Brand Gradient (Royal Blue to Cyan).
 Navigation Links: Home, About, Projects, Blog.
 Interaction: Simple hover effect (e.g., grey pill background).
 Theme Toggle: Sun/Moon icon (placed between the Blog link and the CTA).
 CTA Button: "Download CV" (or Resume).
 Style: Pill-shaped button (nested inside the navbar), Ocean Blue background,
  White text.Mobile Behavior:Collapses to just the Logo (Left) and a Hamburger Menu Icon 
  (Right).Menu expands downwards from the capsule when clicked.
  B. The Hero Section (The Hook)Layout: Split Screen (Text on the Left, Visuals on the Right).
  Background: Pure White (#ffffff).Left Content (The Copy):Headline: "Data Scientist turned Full-Stack Engineer.
  "Sub-headline: "Hi, I'm Souleimane. I leverage my background in AI and Deep Learning to build scalable, data-driven web solutions using Django and React."Tagline: "Master's in Data Science • Medical Imaging & NLP • Web Development"Action Buttons:Primary: "View My Work" (Pill-shaped, Ocean Blue).Secondary: "Visit Blog" (Outline style).Right Content (The Visual):Composition: A Layered 3D effect.Base Layer: A fluid, organic "Blob" shape colored with the Brand Gradient (#2563eb $\to$ #06b6d4).Top Layer: A clean, cut-out portrait (background removed) placed over the blob to create depth.Animation Strategy (Framer Motion):Entrance (On Load):Navbar: Slides down from the top with a soft bounce.Text: Staggered "Fade Up" effect (Headline $\to$ Sub-headline $\to$ Buttons).Visual: Gradient Blob scales up (expands from 0% to 100%), followed by the Portrait fading in.Idle Loop (Continuous):Blob: Slowly morphs shape or "breathes" to add life.Portrait: "Levitation" effect (moves up and down slightly/slowly) to separate it from the background.Interactions: Buttons lift slightly and cast a larger shadow on hover.
